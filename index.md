# Pull!

**Trap & Skeet League Scoring**

Pull! is a complete scoring and management app for trap and skeet shooting leagues — built for league secretaries and team captains who want to leave the paper spreadsheets behind.

## Features

- League setup for trap and skeet with discipline-aware defaults
- Round-robin season scheduler with site and flight optimization
- Per-shot or totals-only scoring; captains submit, secretaries approve
- Live standings, handicaps, classifications, and improvement scores
- Reports on-screen and as PDF: standings, results, leaders, schedule
- iCloud sync and league sharing across devices
- Privacy-first: your data lives on your device and in your own iCloud account — no third-party servers, no ads, no tracking
