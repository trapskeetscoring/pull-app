# Pull! Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an account to use Pull!?

No. There is no account or sign-up. Each device has its own identity, and league members can optionally protect their identity with a 4-digit PIN.

### Does Pull! work without iCloud?

Yes. Pull! works fully offline. iCloud is only required if you want to sync a league across multiple devices or share a league with other people.

### How do I share a league with my team?

From the league screen, open the share menu. Both you and each recipient must be signed into iCloud. You can set each collaborator to read-only or read-write.

### Can I export my data?

Yes. From the Reports section, you can export the season as PDF, CSV, or JSON.

### How are scores corrected after submission?

The league secretary can edit any approved score directly. Captains submit score sheets that the secretary reviews and approves — a full audit trail is preserved.

### My league isn't showing on my other device — what's wrong?

Confirm both devices are signed into the same iCloud account and that iCloud Drive is enabled. The cloud icon at the top of the league list shows sync status; tap it for diagnostics.

## Reporting a Bug

Please include:

- iPhone or iPad model and iOS version
- A description of what you were doing when the problem occurred
- The exact text of any error message
- Screenshots if applicable
