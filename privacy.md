# Privacy Policy for Pull!

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Overview

Pull! is a shooting league scoring and management app for trap and skeet leagues. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is stored, and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

Pull! stores only data that you enter directly into the app:

- **League information** — league name, rules, and settings
- **Team and member information** — team names, member names, email addresses, and phone numbers
- **Scores and statistics** — weekly shooting scores, handicaps, and season records
- **Schedule information** — match dates, site assignments, and matchups

## How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in two places:

- **On your device** — a local file in your app's Documents folder. This data stays on your device and is not accessible to other apps.
- **iCloud (optional)** — if you are signed into iCloud on your device, data is synced through Apple's CloudKit service to your other devices and optionally shared with other app users you explicitly invite. iCloud data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.
- Data is only shared with other app users when you explicitly choose to share a league using the in-app sharing feature.
- Shared leagues are transmitted securely through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device and in iCloud until you delete it. Deleting the app removes local data. iCloud data can be removed through your iCloud account settings.

## Children's Privacy

Pull! does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is intended for use by adult shooting league administrators and members.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
