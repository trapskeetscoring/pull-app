## What is Pull!?

Pull! is a complete scoring and management app for trap and skeet shooting
leagues — built for league secretaries and team captains who want to leave the
paper spreadsheets behind. It runs the season schedule, captures weekly scores
(per-shot or totals-only), computes handicaps and standings, and syncs across
devices via iCloud.

Pull! is available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.
